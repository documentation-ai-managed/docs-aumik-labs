### Overview
Partner with established US colocation providers to resell their services. You add value through India DR capabilities, managed services, and 24/7 support.

### Step 1: Choose Partnership Model

| Model | Description | Your Investment | Revenue Share |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Reseller** | Buy wholesale, sell retail | $0-10K deposit | You keep 15-30% margin |
| **Referral Partner** | Send leads, earn commission | $0 | 5-15% of first year |
| **Channel Partner** | Co-branded solution | $5-25K | 20-40% margin |
| **White Label** | Your brand, their infra | $25-100K | 30-50% margin |

**Recommendation:** Start as **Reseller** → Graduate to **White Label**
