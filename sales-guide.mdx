## Sales Process

### 1. Qualify (BANT)
- **Budget:** Can they afford $500+/mo?
- **Authority:** Decision maker?
- **Need:** Real pain point?
- **Timeline:** When do they need to move?

### 2. Discovery Call (30 min)
- Current setup (5 min)
- Pain points (10 min)
- Requirements (10 min)
- Next steps (5 min)

### 3. Proposal
- Option A: Basic (lowest price)
- Option B: Recommended (best value)
- Option C: Premium (full solution)

### 4. Close
- Assumptive: "If we start Monday, does that work?"
- Trial: "How about a 1-month pilot at reduced price?"
